Initial Render Constraints

By default, we do not animate during the initial render of any of the liquid fire helpers. But you can choose to.

onInitialRender

Include this in a transition rule (this.onInitialRender()) to make the rule match only during the initial render of one of our template helpers.

includingInitialRender

Include this in a transition rule (this.includingInitialRender()) to make the rule match during the initial render and subsequent renders.

These two methods are different from all the other rule constraints in the sense that they remove a constraint that is present by default rather than imposing new constraints.
